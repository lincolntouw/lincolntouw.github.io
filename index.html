About Me

Hi! My name is Lincoln, although I'm more commonly known online as Pixel. I’m a 15-year-old self-taught programmer who first entered the technological world back in 2017, at just the age of 7. It all started with Scratch projects I would make on my mom's laptop when I got home from school. This eventually led to me diving into doing scripting on Roblox.

When I was 11, my teacher even gave me the opportunity to teach basic programming to my Grade 5 class during a full school block once a week. It was a fun way to share what I’d learned as a kid, and it helped me realize how much I enjoy teaching and explaining tech to others; even if they didn’t always get my jokes.

These days, I love solving problems and using code as a creative tool. I mainly work with Lua, JavaScript, and Python, and I’m always experimenting with new ideas - sometimes too many!

While I haven’t released a big project (yet!), I’ve built all kinds of systems, tools, libraries, and mechanics, mostly in Roblox. I’m aiming to become a successful Roblox developer and hopefully make it to RDC by the time I hit 18. In the long term, I wish to launch my own cybersecurity company to make the web more secure, as well as making security easier to implement; especially for young developers such as myself.

If you would like to learn more about my work, keep scrolling!!
